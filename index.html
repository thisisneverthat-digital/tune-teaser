TUNE

Located in Seong-su, the emerging epicenter for subcultures
and communities of Seoul, TUNE is a creative concept
store and community hub with its roots in the 90's culture of
basketball, music and art.

Coming from the industry leading Korean brand
thisisneverthat, TUNE marks its beginning backed by
creative know-hows and infrastructurethat we've built over
the past decade.

As community leaders, we are always contemplating
as to how we can act our part in art, culture as well as the fashion industry.

Planting seeds of inspiration for new generations and at the same time leaving progressive impacts in the market for all to witness, our aim is to elevate the culture
and appreciation towards it of all those within.
